Study Centres

Vivekananda Study Centre

Transferring Timeless Truths to Young India, the Centre aims to enable students to envision and build a stronger nation by adopting the tenets of Swami Vivekananda, a timeless leader.

Gandhi Study Centre

The Centre promotes the vision and ideals of Mahatma Gandhi. Premised on the belief that their philosophies have perennial relevance;

Ambedkar Study Centre

These centres promote an awareness in the student community regarding the thoughts and vision as well as continued relevance of the three great architects of modern India.

Basava Study Centre

Basava Study Centre is established with the intention of imbibing the universal message and practices of Basavanna to enrich the students, personality.
